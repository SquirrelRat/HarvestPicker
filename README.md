# Harvest Picker

An ExileAPI plugin that helps you optimize your Harvest encounters by calculating the most profitable crop rotation sequences.

---

## Features

*   **Optimal Harvest Sequence:** Dynamically calculates the best sequence of plots to harvest, maximizing your yield based on current market prices or SSF priorities.

  <img width="186" height="129" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6f45d55b-67a2-443a-87c2-aef5853b387a" />

---

*   **Intelligent Value Calculation:** Supports both trade league (using poe.ninja prices) and SSF (prioritizing high-tier monsters) evaluation modes to suit your playstyle.
*   **In-Game Overlay:** Provides a clear and concise overlay directly in-game, displaying the optimal harvest order and expected returns.

---

<img width="336" height="361" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/66cad161-2b6d-4057-84c9-6885e3f56e84" />

*   **Customizable Settings:** Fine-tune various parameters like upgrade chances, plant values, and display options to personalize your Harvest strategy.
